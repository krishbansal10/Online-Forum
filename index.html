    Why should I join GDSC?

    Answered by User101

    GDSC is a Google-backed global community. You should join the Google Developer Student Club of your college to build connections with student developers across the world. Most importantly, you will stay updated on opportunities like competitions, internships, scholarships, hackathons and much more.

    How to improve my coding skills?

    Answered by User106

    To improve your coding skills, you should first focus on only one language and then master the basics of the language first. Then, you should start solving questions on daily basis from platforms like leetcode. The most important thing is to be consistent and never give up.
